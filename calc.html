Why I made this calculator

I once got overcharged at Festival Mall when a parking attendant “miscalculated” my stay. Rushed and unable to argue, I paid more than I should have. That moment made me realize how easy it is to be taken advantage of, so I built this simple time tracker. Now I can always check the exact hours and minutes myself—no more convenient miscalculations.
